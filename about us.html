Our Story


Ayam Gepuk AG

Ayam Gepuk AG, founded in 2010 by culinary enthusiasts Agnes and Gustavo, began as a small street food stall in Jakarta, Indonesia. Inspired by their love for traditional Indonesian flavors and their desire to create a unique culinary experience, they decided to specialize in Ayam Gepuk, a popular local dish featuring crispy fried chicken with a flavorful spice blend.Rasai KepedasanDi Setiap Suapan

Today, Ayam Gepuk AG is not just a local favorite but has also gained recognition on a national level. The company continues to evolve, introducing new variations of Ayam Gepuk and exploring innovative ways to share their culinary passion with customers. With a vision to bring the unique flavors of Ayam Gepuk to global markets, Ayam Gepuk AG remains dedicated to its roots of delivering authentic, crispy goodness to food lovers around the world.

Vision
